# Group Project: Group Organization

## Overview

Continue to connect with your group members via MS Teams asynchronously. You will be working with a group on a common development project using Next.js through week 5 of the course. This initial group assignment is to introduce yourself to your team, identify the strengths of each group member, review the project's purpose and functional specifications, and to set up effective communication channels.

Purposes:

- Develop Software Development Skills: Build a web application using a full stack technology stack.
- Develop as an Effective group member: Improve professionalism and ability to collaborate.
- Teach one Another: Love, serve, and teach one another.

## Instructions

- Continue to connect with your group members as they join the MS Teams group channel.
- Share information that will help you decide on specific meeting times, collaborative technology use, and group roles based upon individual strengths.
- Confirm the exact meeting times and virtual platform (MS Teams, Zoom, etc.).
- Designate someone to be the group lead for the virtual meeting next week. The group leader assignment will be rotated through the group each week.
- Assign the group lead the task of reminding group members via MS Teams, email, WhatsApp, etc., a day before the meeting date and time. Have them plan on including the meeting agenda and about any specific instructions or materials participants need to prepare.
- Individually study the group project description in order to be prepared for next week's group work and meeting.
- Respect participants' time and ensure meetings stay focused and productive. Prepare for the meeting as required and share your video by camera during the meeting as well as your voice.

## Submission

Submit the following information in Canvas:

- The number and/or name.
- The group's meeting time.
- The name of the group leader for next week's meeting.
- The instructor will verify that you have signed up for a group channel in MS Teams.
